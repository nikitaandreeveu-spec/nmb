## Slide Content Details

### **Core Strategic Pivots**
*   **Value Proposition:** Shifting from "access to Claude" to **guaranteed business outcomes** and **sovereign AI** [1]. Anthropic now differentiates as the "Trusted Western Frontier Model" for high-security environments [2].
*   **Revenue Model:** Moving away from seat-based subscriptions toward **outcome-based pricing** to ensure revenue covers high compute costs and aligns with client ROI [1].
*   **Technology Architecture:** Implementing **efficiency-first and Green AI architectures** to achieve 3–4x efficiency improvements per generation, mitigating the "Red" stress factor of high resource consumption [1, 2].
*   **Organizational Moat:** Building a **regulatory moat** by productizing safety and compliance (e.g., evaluation suites) to master frameworks like the EU AI Act [2].
