## Critical Path & Decision Gates

### **Gate 1: Q2 2026 — Pilot Validation**
**Decision:** Do outcome-based pricing models work with real customers?
- Required: 3+ pilots with documented 15%+ ROI
- If YES → Proceed to Phase 1 acceleration
- If NO → Revise pricing model; engage consulting partners for co-design

### **Gate 2: Q4 2026 — Phase 1 Success Validation**
**Decision:** Is organizational and market readiness adequate for Phase 2 scaling?
- Required: $15M+ ARR on outcome contracts; 150+ customers; 3+ verticals live
- If YES → Authorize Phase 2 acceleration (500+ enterprise customers)
- If NO → Extend Phase 1; add Q1 2027 as transition quarter

### **Gate 3: Q2 2027 — Scaling Confirmation**
**Decision:** Is the enterprise sales machine sustainable at scale?
- Required: 300+ customers; 70% consulting partner pipeline; 25%+ NRR
- If YES → Authorize Phase 3 consolidation; expand to 10+ verticals
- If NO → Pause new vertical launches; focus on existing customer expansion

### **Gate 4: Q4 2027 — Market Leadership Confirmation**
**Decision:** Can Anthropic achieve uncontested geopolitical and compliance positioning?
- Required: EU AI Act reference implementation; 5+ allied government deployments; $1B+ government revenue
- If YES → Position for $30B+ revenue target by end of 2028
- If NO → Reset government strategy; focus on domestic US market

## Dependencies & Risk Mitigation

### **External Dependencies**
| Dependency | Risk | Mitigation |
|------------|------|-----------|
| **Consulting Partner Availability** | Tier-1 integrators divest from AI initiatives | Multi-partner strategy; co-investment incentives |
| **Government Regulatory Clarity** | EU AI Act evolves; new regulations emerge | Continuous regulatory tracking; proactive compliance investments |
| **Compute Supply** | GPU shortage constraints training and inference | Multi-vendor partnerships (Cerebras, Groq); long-term GPU supply agreements |
| **Geopolitical Stability** | Export controls, sanctions restrict markets | Distributed sovereign compute architecture; allied government partnerships |

### **Internal Dependencies**
| Dependency | Risk | Mitigation |
|------------|------|-----------|
| **Talent & Organizational Scaling** | Hiring market remains tight; competitive talent loss | Equity packages; public-benefit mission attraction; internship pipeline |
| **Product Innovation Velocity** | Vertical solutions take longer than planned | Modular agent architecture; partner co-development; internal product acceleration |
| **Operational Excellence** | SLA misses damage customer trust | Outcome guarantees; risk-sharing contracts; dedicated customer success teams |
