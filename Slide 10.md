# Slide 10: 3-Tiered Implementation Roadmap (Aligned to Slide 8)
**Design Directive:** Render with **Anthropic Beige (#F5F2ED)** backgrounds and **Safety Green (#4A7C59)** accents. Each tier shows 3-phase progression: Foundation (2026) → Scaling (2027) → Market Leadership (2028).

---

## TIER 1: Revenue Model Redesign (Outcome-Based Pricing)

### Phase 1: Foundation (2026)
**Q1-Q2:** Finalize pricing framework & run 10-15 pilots with enterprise customers
- Define ROI measurement methodologies and SLA performance guarantees
- Sign outcome-based contracts with 5 healthcare, 3 legal, 2 financial services customers
- Achieve 80%+ NPS and 15-25% documented ROI in pilots
- **Success Metric:** $15M+ ARR from outcome-based contracts by Q4 2026

### Phase 2: Scaling (2027)
**Q1-Q4:** Transition 300-400 enterprises to outcome-based contracts
- Scale enterprise sales team to 50+ account executives
- Drive 70%+ of pipeline through consulting partners (Accenture, Deloitte, McKinsey)
- Achieve $200K-500K ACV and 25%+ net revenue retention
- **Success Metric:** $100-150M ARR by end of 2027

### Phase 3: Market Leadership (2028)
**Q1-Q4:** Establish outcome-based pricing as dominant revenue model
- 500+ enterprises on multi-year outcome contracts
- 60-65% gross margins through efficiency optimization
- Outcome-based revenue representing 70% of enterprise revenue
- **Success Metric:** $200M+ ARR from outcome-based contracts

## TIER 2: Sustainability Positioning (Green AI & Carbon-Aware Operations)

### Phase 1: Foundation (2026)
**Q1-Q3:** Deploy carbon-aware scheduling and achieve 3-4x efficiency gains
- Build carbon-aware job scheduling system prioritizing low-carbon hours
- Release Green AI v1.0 with 2-3x efficiency improvements vs. baseline
- Partner with renewable-focused cloud providers (AWS, Google, Azure)
- **Success Metric:** 30% reduction in per-inference compute costs by Q3 2026

### Phase 2: Scaling (2027)
**Q1-Q4:** Release Green AI v2.0 with 5x efficiency and market as premium differentiator
- Achieve 5x efficiency vs. baseline; cost reduced 45%
- Launch sustainability dashboards for enterprise customers
- Market carbon-neutral positioning to ESG-focused enterprises
- **Success Metric:** $8-12M premium revenue from ESG customers by end 2027

### Phase 3: Market Leadership (2028)
**Q1-Q4:** Establish Anthropic as sustainability leaders in enterprise AI
- Green AI v3.0 achieving 6-7x efficiency
- Carbon-neutral operations across all deployments
- Sustainability commitment drives 15-20% pricing premium
- **Success Metric:** 40% of enterprise customers self-identify on sustainability criteria

## TIER 3: Vertical Solutions (Healthcare, Legal, Finance, Public Sector)

### Phase 1: Foundation (2026)
**Q1-Q3:** Launch 3 vertical agents with specialized compliance frameworks
- Healthcare: Deploy compliance agents for clinical documentation, coding, privacy (HIPAA)
- Legal: Launch discovery and contract analysis agents (eDiscovery compliant)
- Finance: Deploy audit workflow automation and regulatory reporting (MiFID II)
- **Success Metric:** 2K+ customers across 3 verticals by Q3 2026; 40% MoM growth

### Phase 2: Scaling (2027)
**Q1-Q4:** Expand to 8-10 verticals and reach $200M+ revenue run rate
- Launch 5-7 additional verticals (insurance, engineering, public sector, ESG, etc.)
- Establish vertical-specific consulting partnerships
- Build domain expertise centers for each vertical
- **Success Metric:** 10K+ customers across 8-10 verticals; $200M+ revenue by end 2027

### Phase 3: Market Leadership (2028)
**Q1-Q4:** Dominate regulated verticals with best-in-class compliance and outcomes
- 12+ specialized vertical solutions deployed globally
- 50K+ customers across verticals
- Vertical solutions representing 40% of total enterprise revenue
- **Success Metric:** $500M+ annual revenue from vertical solutions
